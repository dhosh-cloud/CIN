# Data Deletion Request

**Crave It Now**

If you would like your account and all associated data permanently deleted, please contact us directly at:

**Email:** dale@hoshooley.com

Please include the following in your request:
- The email address associated with your Crave It Now account
- Subject line: **"Data Deletion Request"**

We will process your request and confirm deletion within **30 days**.

Data deleted includes:
- Your account profile (name, email, profile picture)
- Saved meal plans and grocery lists
- Dietary preferences and settings
- Family group memberships
- Any scanned meal card images stored on our servers
