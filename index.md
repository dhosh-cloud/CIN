# Privacy Policy

**Crave It Now**
**Effective Date:** April 18, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Crave It Now ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Crave It Now mobile application (the "App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** When you sign in using Google or Microsoft, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your chosen identity provider.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Camera Data:** With your permission, the App uses your device camera to scan meal cards and barcodes. Images captured are transmitted to our servers for processing and are not permanently stored beyond what is necessary to complete the scan.
- **Usage Data:** We may collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used, actions taken, and error logs.

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Google Sign-In / Microsoft Sign-In:** We receive basic profile information (name, email, profile picture) from your identity provider when you authenticate.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, operate, and maintain the App
- Process meal card and barcode scans using AI-powered analysis
- Manage your account and preferences
- Maintain family group features and shared content
- Generate personalised grocery lists and meal plans
- Communicate with you about your account or the App
- Monitor and improve App performance and security
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 3. Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device camera solely for the following purposes:
- **Meal Card Scanning:** Capturing images of recipe cards to extract ingredient and cooking information via AI analysis
- **Barcode Scanning:** Reading product barcodes to identify grocery items

Camera images are processed on our secure servers and are not shared with third parties except as required to perform the scan (e.g., AI processing services). Images are deleted from our servers once processing is complete unless you explicitly save the result.

We do not use the camera to collect any other type of data, and camera access is never activated without your knowledge.

---

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

- **Service Providers:** We may share data with trusted third-party providers who assist in operating the App (e.g., cloud hosting, AI processing). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.
- **Family Groups:** If you join or create a family group within the App, certain information (such as your name and shared lists) is visible to other group members.
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
- **Business Transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App's services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us (see Section 10).

Scanned images are deleted from our servers immediately after processing is complete unless you choose to save the result to your account.

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and secure server storage. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the address below.

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data
- **Portability:** Request a machine-readable copy of your data
- **Objection:** Object to certain types of data processing
- **Withdrawal of Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent (e.g., revoking camera permission via device settings)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **dale@hoshooley.com**.
